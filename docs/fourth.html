As I spoke about carnatic music in my previous page, I would like to give some details about this form of music.

This form of music is more prevalent in South India. There are many great singers in this form. I admire MS.Subbalakshmi garu, Nithyasri Mahadevan, Sudha Raghunathan and many other legendary singers. I also like Kunnakudi vaidyanadhan's violin, Rajesh Vaidhya' veena etc.

Carnatic music has many ragas and thalas There are about 72 ragas well-known ragas and many others.They are mainly classified into Janaka ragas(Parent) and Janya ragas(Derived).
